About

I’m a full-time inbound marketing specialist with expertise in content marketing, communications, and multimedia production. I’m interested in writing factual stories about the subaltern, technology, rural India, female empowerment, and global health.

I love creating content- be it textual, visual, or graphic.

In some ways, the field of content creation has rescued me. :)

What started as a part-time job in college (writing SEO-friendly content for websites) has now snowballed into a full-time career for me.

In my current role, I work as an inbound specialist and Content Editor for AdPushup, a SaaS company in India. I have assisted the marketing team by increasing organic traffic on AdPushup’s blog, improving CTRs on paid ads, introducing innovative forms of CTAs, email marketing including newsletters and digital gifts, and writing press releases and brand articles under C-suite people’s names.

Apart from full-time work, I’ve developed a keen interest in photography and videography in the past 1 year. Even though I cannot venture out because of the pandemic, I have fallen in love with the art of post-processing. With every passing day, I’m becoming more and more skilled in Adobe Creatove Cloud.

As for my education, I graduated with an Honours degree in English Literature from Delhi University. College had a remarkable impact on my career trajectory by providing me with interesting avenues to schools of thought such as feminism, modernism, subaltern activism, amongst many others.

As I’ve grown older, I’ve developed a deep interest in storytelling, especially editorial content that talks about marginalized communities, environment, technology, and public health. I’m inspired by the work of journalists like P. Sainath, social reformers like Kailash Satyarthi, and Members of Parliament like Atishi Marlena.

I believe that the art of journalism and storytelling should be supported by everyone. Awareness is just as important as actions.

As for my hobbies, I like watching sports, especially football. I’ve always loved reading books since I was a child and I still continue to read whenever I get the time.
